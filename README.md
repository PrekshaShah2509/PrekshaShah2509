# Preksha Shah

**Head of Engineering | LLM Systems & Production AI | Engineering Leadership**

Helping engineering leaders bring clarity to complex technology and delivery decisions, before execution makes them expensive to change.

I work at the intersection of production AI systems and engineering leadership. Architecture decisions, system trade-offs, and what it takes to ship reliable software at scale. On the advisory side: decision-level guidance for when ambiguity is high, stakes are real, and clarity is required before resources are committed.

---

## What I Do

**Engineering Leadership & Production AI**
Architecture decisions, LLM systems reliability, token economics, context architecture, fallback design, and the organizational signals that hide inside technical problems. Production AI is not a tooling question. It is a systems design question.

**Advisory & Strategy**
Technology roadmaps, AI/ML feasibility, architecture reviews, and technical due diligence for product-led and AI-first companies. I show up when decisions are ambiguous, stakes are high, and clarity is required before execution commits resources.

**Execution & Delivery**
Direct ownership of outcomes, not recommendations handed off to someone else. I show up when delivery is at risk and accountability matters more than advice.

---

## How I Work

**Context first.** Solutions shaped by constraints, teams, and business context, not generic best practices.

**Pragmatic architecture.** The right trade-off between technical debt, speed, and maintainability depends on where the team is, not where the textbook says they should be.

**Execution clarity.** Strategy without execution clarity is just planning.

**Build capability, not dependency.** The goal is always to leave the team stronger than it was.

## Open Source Work

### [Production Ready Checklist](https://github.com/PrekshaShah2509/production-ready-checklist)
A comprehensive, battle-tested checklist for deploying applications safely and reliably. Covers seven domains: Security, Performance, Testing, Monitoring and Logging, Database, Deployment, and Post-Deployment. Includes templates for deployment plans, rollback procedures, and post-deployment verification. MIT licensed.

### [Atomic Structure Demo](https://github.com/PrekshaShah2509/atomic-structure-demo)
Interactive TypeScript and React demo illustrating Atomic Design architecture. Shows how components compose from atoms to templates in a structured, maintainable hierarchy. Built for teams learning to apply design system thinking in practice.
